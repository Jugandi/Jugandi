About us

Our Main Goal

To include everyone in a community that doesnt judge, at the same time providing a safe and enjoyable environment where each person feels at home

Who we are

Our club is run by volunteers for volunteers. Learn new skills, meet like-minded people and enjoy one of the closest beaches to the city.

Everyone is welcome

Everyone no matter the age, gender, or beleif, everyone is welcome to participate in all out activities without any hassle.

Facilities and classes

There are a huge variety of facilitiesa available to memebers, such as surf boards, boats. There are also many calsses that run multiple times a week all run by volunteers to teach you various things.
